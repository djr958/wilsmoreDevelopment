Preparing For Sale!

Three double storey, 4 bedroom, 2.5 bathroom strata titled properties are being built on land overlooking the Jolimont Primary School bushland in Wilsmore Street, Daglish.

They will be offered for sale when complete around September 2018.

Please contact us [link to the Contact Us page] If you would like to be advised when the properties are about to be sold.

Built to a very high standard by Studio8 Builders & Designers [Link to Studio 8 website, to open on a new page ] each property has:

  • The main bedroom, with ensuite, downstairs
  • An upstairs balcony that overlooks the Jolimont Primary School bushland
  • Pacific blackbutt wood flooring in the kitchen, the upstairs and downstairs living areas and on the stairs
  • Carpet in the bedrooms
  • Tiles in the bathrooms, toilets and laundry
  • Reverse cycle ducted air-conditioning
  • Overhead fans in two bedrooms
  • 5kW solar system
  • Whirlybird on the roof
  • A double lockup garage
  • Slimline 3-in-1 combo heat / fan / light in the ensuite bathroom
  • Security system
  • Security flywire screens to external doors and windows, except awning windows which have basic flywire screens
  • First floor concrete slab
